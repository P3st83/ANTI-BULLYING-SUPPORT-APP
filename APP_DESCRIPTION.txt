PROJECT SUMMARY
===============================================================================
A comprehensive digital platform designed to provide emotional support, 
anonymous reporting, and educational resources for children and teens (ages 7-16) 
experiencing bullying. The app creates a safe, accessible environment where 
young users can track emotions, report incidents, learn coping strategies, 
and receive 24/7 AI-powered support.

TARGET AUDIENCE
===============================================================================
PRIMARY USERS:
- Children and teens aged 7-16 experiencing bullying
- Students seeking emotional support and guidance
- Young people who need a safe space to express feelings

SECONDARY USERS:
- Parents monitoring their child's emotional wellbeing
- Teachers and school counselors
- Mental health professionals
- Educational institutions

KEY FEATURES & FUNCTIONALITY
===============================================================================

1. DAILY MOOD TRACKING
   - Simple emoji-based mood selection interface
   - Intensity levels (1-5 scale) for each emotion
   - Visual analytics showing mood patterns over time
   - Streak tracking to encourage daily engagement
   - Motivational quotes that change daily

2. AI-POWERED CHAT SUPPORT
   - 24/7 emotional support chatbot
   - Age-appropriate responses tailored for children
   - Crisis detection and emergency response protocols
   - Content safety filtering and moderation
   - Conversation starters and guided discussions
   - Personal information protection

3. ANONYMOUS REPORTING SYSTEM
   - Safe incident reporting with optional identity disclosure
   - Severity classification (low, medium, high, critical)
   - Automatic escalation for serious cases
   - Evidence attachment capabilities
   - Follow-up tracking and support
   - Integration with school systems

4. LEARNING CENTER
   - Age-appropriate educational content about bullying
   - Interactive activities and quizzes
   - Progress tracking with achievement badges
   - Categories: prevention, emotional intelligence, confidence building
   - Video content and visual learning materials
   - Downloadable resources for offline access

5. EMERGENCY SUPPORT SYSTEM
   - One-tap emergency contact button
   - Crisis hotline integration
   - Immediate access to professional resources
   - Emergency keyword detection in conversations
   - Automatic alerts to trusted adults (with permission)

6. COMMUNITY FEATURES
   - Moderated success stories and testimonials
   - Peer support through anonymous sharing
   - Upvoting system for helpful content
   - Safe community guidelines enforcement
